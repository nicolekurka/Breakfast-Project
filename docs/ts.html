Having Some Trouble?

If you accidentally burnt yourself...

You might have touched a pan that is still hot while moving it to the sink to wash.

Treat the wound immediately.

  1. Run cold water over the affected area.
  2. Take the Neosporin from the junk drawer under the sink and apply it to the wound
  3. Depending on the size of the burn, decide if you want to use a BandAid or wrap it with gauze. Both of these supplies are located with the Neosporin.
